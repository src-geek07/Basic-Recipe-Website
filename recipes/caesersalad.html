Caesar Salad !!

A classic Caesar Salad recipe with crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy homemade croutons, fresh parmesan cheese, and a light Caesar dressing for when you want to impress your dinner guests.

Ingredients :

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Crisp Croutons
  • Homemade Caesar Dressing

Directions :

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add your prepared romaine lettuce, top with croutons and parmesan.
  2. Just before serving, drizzle with your dressing, and toss gently to coat the lettuce and croutons.
